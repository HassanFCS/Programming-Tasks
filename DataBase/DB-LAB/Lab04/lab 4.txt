DBS
Exercise of Select, Where, Update, Delete commands along with And/Or operations.
You will use the same Company schema that has been shared with you all in Lab 02.
Execute the following queries.
1.	Retrieve the birth date and address of the employee(s) whose name is ‘John B. Smith’.
2.	Retrieve the name and address of all employees who work for the ‘Research’ department.
3.	For every project located in ‘stafford’, list the project number, the controlling department number, and the department manager’s last name. 
4.	Retrieve the salary of every employee and all distinct salary values.
5.	Make a list of all project numbers for projects that involve an employee whose last name is ‘Smith’  as a worker of the project.
6.	Find all employees who were born during the 1950s.
7.	Retrieve all employees in department no 5 whose salary is between $30,000 and $40,000.
8.	Give all employees in department no. 5 a 10 percent raise in salary.
9.	Delete the works_on tuple with Essn = ‘999887777’ and Pno = 10
10.	Update the salary of the employee with Ssn = ‘99988777’ to 28000.
11.	Update the Dno of the employee tuple with Ssn = ‘999887777’ to 1.
12.	Modify the Super_ssn attribute of the employee tuple with Ssn = ‘999887777’ to ‘943775543’ and observe the result. 
13.	Modify the hours attribute of the works_on tuple with Essn = ‘999887777’ and Pno = 10 to ‘5.0’. Observe the results.
14.	Delete the project tuple with Pname = ‘ProductX’.
15.	Delete the employee tuple with Ssn = ‘987654321’.
16.	Delete the works_on tuple with Essn = ‘333445555’.
